# MASAGI HV — 7-Day Organic Launch Plan (LinkedIn + Instagram)

**Campaign name:** "Lihat Bisnismu dari Atas" (See Your Business From Above)
**Summary:** A 7-day, near-zero-budget organic launch that takes growing Indonesian SMEs
from "I can't see my own numbers" to booking a MASAGI HV demo — founder-led, Bahasa-first
on Instagram, authority-building on LinkedIn.

**Primary objective:** 10 qualified demo conversations (DM / email / WhatsApp) within the 7 days.
**Secondary:** 100+ combined new followers, 30+ visits to masagi.id, a reusable content engine.

---

## 1. Target audience

> Owner / co-founder / finance lead of a growing Indonesian SME (5–50 staff, typically
> trading, F&B, services, construction, creative) who runs the business from a bank app,
> WhatsApp and scattered spreadsheets, finds out profit weeks after month-end, and is
> curious-but-skeptical about AI. Discovers tools via Instagram Reels, WhatsApp groups and
> LinkedIn; cares most about cash, cost, and not hiring more admin staff.

- **Instagram** = the owner, emotional pain + demo visuals, Bahasa Indonesia.
- **LinkedIn** = the same owner in "professional mode" + finance managers, consultants,
  accountants who refer tools. English–Indonesian mix, founder voice.

## 2. Key messages

1. **Pain:** "You know today's sales. You don't know last month's profit." Running blind
   kills growing SMEs — decisions made on feelings, not numbers.
2. **Product:** MASAGI HV = one live helicopter view of the whole business — every account,
   every rupiah, down to a single journal line. IDR-first, made in Indonesia.
3. **AI angle:** AI runs the repetitive back office (bank mutasi → booked journals, reports
   assemble themselves). Cut the cost of manual work — point your people at the market.
   *Always* pair with human-in-the-loop: "AI does the typing, you keep the decisions."
4. **Tailored:** custom-built around each company, not a template. Pilot clients shape it.

**Proof points:** 10-minute mutasi-to-journal demo (screen recording), live dashboard,
2-way Excel, self-hosted data ownership.

## 3. Rules of the game (minimal budget)

- **Founder-led:** all LinkedIn posts from the founder profile (10× reach of a new company
  page); company page reshares. IG posts from @masagi brand account, founder appears in Reels.
- **First hour rule:** reply to every comment within 60 min; spend 15 min engaging with
  target-audience posts before and after publishing.
- **Distribution > creation:** each day, share the IG post to Stories, cross-post the Reel
  to WhatsApp Status + 2–3 relevant WhatsApp/Telegram SME groups (as a human, not spam).
- **Zero-cost assets:** everything is screen recordings of the live product + Canva
  carousels in brand style (white/charcoal, red accent, green logo).
- **Boost only what already works:** total paid budget ≤ Rp 600.000 —
  Rp 300k on the Day-3 launch Reel, Rp 300k on whichever post performed best by Day 6
  (IG "boost" on Reels; target: Indonesia, 24–45, business/entrepreneur interests).

**Posting times (WIB):** LinkedIn 08:30–09:30 · Instagram feed 11:30–12:30 · Reels 18:30–19:30 · Stories throughout.

## 4. Day-by-day calendar

> perasaan.
>
> Kami membangun sesuatu untuk masalah ini. Rabu ini saya tunjukkan. 🏔
>
> #UKM #UMKM #KeuanganBisnis

**Instagram Reel (15s, text-on-screen, trending calm audio):**

### Day 2 — Selasa · education carousel (authority)
**Instagram carousel (6 slides, brand template):** "5 tanda pembukuan diam-diam menahan
pertumbuhan bisnismu" — 1) laporan jadi > tanggal 15, 2) nggak tahu proyek mana yang rugi,

**LinkedIn (same content as a document/carousel post, founder voice):** intro line +
PDF carousel. CTA: "Besok saya perkenalkan solusinya."
**Story:** countdown sticker to launch.

### Day 3 — Rabu · **LAUNCH** 🚀
**LinkedIn (founder announcement + company page reshare):**

**Instagram Reel (30s product tour, screen recording + founder VO):** dashboard → drill
down → AI bank import → report export. End: "MASAGI HV. Precise by default. Link di bio."
**Boost this Reel: Rp 300k / 3 hari.** Stories: launch sticker, reshare, behind-the-scenes.
Cross-post to WhatsApp Status + SME groups.

### Day 4 — Kamis · feature deep-dive (AI Bank Import)
**Instagram Reel (20s, timer on screen):** "Dari mutasi BCA → jurnal terbukukan.
⏱ 10 menit." Paste mutasi, AI parse, duplikat ke-flag, approve, selesai. Caption: "Kerjaan
sore admin, selesai sebelum kopi dingin. AI yang ngetik, kamu yang approve. #AIuntukUKM"
**LinkedIn (founder, contrarian angle):**
> Karyawan "termurah" yang bisa kamu rekrut tahun ini bukan manusia.
> Input mutasi, jurnal berulang, laporan bulanan — itu bukan pekerjaan yang menumbuhkan
> bisnis, itu biaya. Kami memindahkannya ke AI, dan mengarahkan orangnya ke penagihan dan
> pelanggan. Bukan mengurangi orang — mengarahkan ulang jam kerjanya. (thread: cara kerjanya)
**Story:** quiz — "Berapa jam per minggu adminmu input mutasi manual?" (<2 / 2–5 / >5 😱)

### Day 5 — Jumat · trust (human-in-the-loop)
**Instagram carousel (5 slides):** "AI yang ngetik. Kamu yang memutuskan." —
apa yang diambil alih AI (parse mutasi, draft jurnal, flag anomali, susun laporan) vs
apa yang tetap di tanganmu (approval, harga, negosiasi, pelanggan). Slide akhir: "Nggak ada
yang terposting tanpa persetujuan manusia."
**LinkedIn (founder):** the 3-step tailored story — Assess → Tailor → Automate; kenapa
kami tidak menjual template, dan kenapa pilot client ikut membentuk produk.
**Story:** DM prompt — "Tanya apa saja soal AI di pembukuan, saya jawab di story besok."

### Day 6 — Sabtu · social proof
**Instagram (testimonial card + metric):** kutipan pilot: "Yang tadinya seharian, sekarang
10 menit." + sebelum/sesudah (jam admin per minggu). Caption cerita singkat.
**LinkedIn (founder, mini case study):** narasi satu pilot: kondisi awal → yang di-automasi
→ hasil 30 hari (waktu closing, jam admin, visibilitas). Angka jujur, skala kecil tak apa.
**Boost: Rp 300k pada post dengan performa terbaik Day 1–5.**
**Story:** jawab pertanyaan yang masuk dari Day 5 (Q&A recap).

### Day 7 — Minggu · CTA + recap
**Instagram Reel/carousel recap:** "Minggu ini kami meluncurkan MASAGI HV 🏔" — 3 klip
terbaik + "Sisa 2 slot pilot. DM 'HV' untuk demo 20 menit."
**LinkedIn (founder):** week-one recap in numbers (reach, percakapan, pelajaran) — build in
public; ulangi tawaran pilot; tag orang-orang yang membantu.
**Story:** link sticker ke masagi.id + Q&A box terakhir "Mau lihat demo? DM 'HV'".

## 7. Risks & mitigations

- **Low reach on brand-new accounts** → founder profile as primary channel; WhatsApp
  groups + communities as distribution; ask 5–10 friendly peers to engage in first hour.
- **"AI will replace jobs" backlash** → every AI post carries the human-in-the-loop line
  ("AI yang ngetik, kamu yang memutuskan"); Day-5 content exists to defuse it.
- **Demo requests exceed capacity** → cap at "3 pilot slots" (scarcity is honest: it's a pilot).
- **A day slips** → priority order: D3 launch > D4 demo > D6 proof; D2/D5 can shift.
