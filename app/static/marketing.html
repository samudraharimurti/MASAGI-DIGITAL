FAQ

Asked often. Answered plainly.

What exactly is the "Helicopter View"?
It is the consolidated layer of MASAGI HV: one dashboard where every entity's revenue, cost, cash and budget realization roll up live, and where you can drop from the group total down to a single journal entry in two clicks.
How is this different from a generic cloud ERP?
Generic ERPs ship fixed workflows and charge you to work around them. MASAGI HV is custom-built: we configure entities, accounts, fields and reports to match how your company already operates, then automate the specific tasks you repeat.
What does "AI-assisted" actually mean here?
AI handles the repetitive operations: it parses bank receipts, mutasi CSV and e-statement PDFs, drafts the journal entries, flags duplicates and unusual amounts, and assembles your recurring reports. Nothing posts without a human approving it — AI does the typing, your team keeps the judgment.
Will AI replace my finance team?
It replaces the typing, not the thinking. The goal is to cut the cost of manual back-office work so the same people can move to what actually grows the business — collections, customers, negotiations. Most growing SMEs don't need fewer people; they need the ones they have pointed at the market.
Can we keep working in Excel?
Yes — Excel is a first-class citizen. Every report exports to .xlsx, and journals, charts of accounts and budgets import from templates you can download in-app.
Where does our data live?
Your choice. Run MASAGI HV self-hosted on your own server for full data residency, or have us manage the deployment. Each database is a self-contained store you can back up as a file.
Which banks do you support for import?
Today: BCA — pasted transfer receipts, mutasi rekening CSV and e-statement PDF, with duplicate detection by reference number. Additional Indonesian and regional banks are prioritized with pilot clients.
