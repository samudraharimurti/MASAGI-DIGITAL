FAQ

Before you buy.

How do I receive the files?
The moment your payment is confirmed by the payment gateway, we create your account automatically and email you a receipt with a sign-in link. Your files are in the "My Downloads" dashboard — available immediately and re-downloadable anytime.
Which payment methods can I use?
QRIS (any Indonesian e-wallet or mobile banking app), BCA virtual account, or credit/debit card — processed through DOKU.
Do download links expire?
Each generated link is valid for 24 hours or 5 downloads — but you can generate a fresh link from your dashboard whenever you like, forever. Your access doesn't expire; only the individual links rotate (that's what keeps them from being shared around).
What does the membership add?
Everything in the toolkit, plus every new template, guide and course we release while your membership is active — we ship new material with every batch of client work.
Can I get an invoice for my company?
Yes — a numbered invoice is generated with every purchase and included in your receipt email and order history.
Refunds?
Digital files can't be un-downloaded, so all sales are final — but if a file is broken or not as described, write to hello@masagi.id and we'll make it right.
